Virtual Environments 2011 Coursework Part 2: CAVE programming

There are two pieces of coursework for this course. Overall the two pieces of coursework are worth 30% of the mark for this course. The exam is worth 70%.

This coursework is is worth 70% of the overall coursework mark. All members of the groups will receive the same mark.

Overview

Your task is to build a VE application which trains a user to perform a specific task. Your application will be marked in terms of its ability to successfully transfer the necessary knowledge to the user so that they can perform the task. Extra credit will be awarded for adding particular interaction to the application, and developing a method to measure the success of the training.

You can make anything engaging with immersive computer graphics and audio. Here are some ideas:

· Tying various kinds of knots

· Building a flat-pack piece of IKEA furniture

· Perform specific acrobatic movements

· Defuse a bomb

· Navigate a complicated environment

· Juggle

· Maths!

Coursework deadline is 4pm on Friday, December 9.
